About Us GökBörü VTC

Founded by Berk at 3 July 2019, GökBörü VTC is the very first Virtual Trucking Company from Turkey that gained Verification. GökBörü VTC's main morale to be created is to have a family. Where you can socialize, hang out and drive trucks together with fellow truckers. True home for a trucker. Even it is originated from Turkey, GökBörü VTC is recently more engaged in international area of the game and looking more drivers across the globe.

Everything we accomplished so far, couldn't be done without our amazing truckers and staff of GökBörü VTC. Thanks to everyone's hard work, we achieved a family spirit with over 100 members.

GökBörü VTC is currently hosting 1 weekly convoy for their members. Apart from those convoys, we join our partner companies' convoy's which makes our schedule very busy with 2-3 convoys in a week. We also make a monthly calendar so our drivers can arrange their schedule accordingly. We always have our Convoy Management Staff ready to manage and host a convoy and we have our own Media Staff where we publish our amazing stories and memories in our Social Media pages.

We also created a Driver's Manager system where our new staff observes the drivers and gives them productive feedback with their driving skills as well as keeping monthly statistics and sharing them within our Discord.

Apart from trucking, thanks to our family spirit, our members also play other games together which increases our family spirit even more.
