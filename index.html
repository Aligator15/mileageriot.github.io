ABOUT US

A little history

Founded by Berk at 3 July 2019, GökBörü VTC is the very first Virtual Trucking Company from Turkey that gained Verification. GökBörü VTC's main morale to be created is to have a family. Where you can socialize, hang out and drive trucks together with fellow truckers. True home for a trucker. Even it is originated from Turkey, GökBörü VTC is recently more engaged in international area of the game and looking more drivers across the globe.
